I’m Cristina, aka 0xStoek, a graffiti writer, illustrator and introvert.

Through my illustrations, I tell stories about graffiti culture and the lessons about fear, impermanence and grit that I’ve learned as a graffiti writer.

I create for the misfits and introverts that haven't found their voice.

I want to encourage them to break the rules and be heard and seen.
